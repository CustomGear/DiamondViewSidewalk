Do You Support the Proposed Sidewalk in Diamondview Estates?

Residents of Diamondview Estates in Carp are asked to weigh in on a planned sidewalk installation that may affect driveway space, parking, and winter safety.

Submit Your Feedback

Why This Matters

Many residents of Diamondview Estates were not clearly informed that a sidewalk was part of the development plan when they purchased their homes. The proposed sidewalk may reduce usable driveway depth and affect day-to-day property access.

For some homes the sidewalk could shorten the driveway by several feet — raising real questions about winter parking, snow clearing responsibility, and roof-slide hazards on narrowed driveways.

This initiative collects resident voices so a clear, organized response can be brought to the City of Ottawa.

Key Concerns Raised by Residents

  • Loss of driveway space and reduced vehicle access
  • Winter parking, snow clearing responsibility, and roof-slide hazards
  • Lack of clear communication or disclosure from the City
  • Homeowners purchased without being told this change was planned
  • General property safety and access concerns

Development Background

The sidewalk is part of the City of Ottawa's approved subdivision plan for Diamondview Estates (File D07-16-22-0017). The utility and infrastructure layout was outlined in official composite plans submitted in 2024. Residents have raised questions about whether this was adequately disclosed during the home-buying process.
